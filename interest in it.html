What is your interest in IT? When did your interest in IT start?

My interest in IT is endless, taking courses on and self-teaching countless field-related topics just for my own enjoyment. Initially I began following a professional career in tech when I left high school in year 10 after designing and programming a video game portfolio and gaining acceptance into the AIE. This was many years before receiving my ADHD diagnosis and so unfortunately class-based learning never worked out. Now, as an adult and with a bit more life experience under my belt, I’m ready to begin pursuing my true passion again.

 

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

I chose RMIT purely for the ability to complete an online pathways program, as it allows me to continue my full-time electrical work which provides for my family and zoo of animals. Eventually I would love to gain entrance to a local (Queensland) online Computer Science degree, however the options for a school leaving tradie are limited so it’s the long way for me!

 

What do you expect to learn during your studies?

I’m hoping to gain more of an understanding surrounding what the IT/tech field entails in the real world. I believe I know which rough end goal I’m aiming for, and I hope to gain the basic tools, skills, and knowledge to start me on my path!
